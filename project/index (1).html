About Annish

We bring out your best, most confident self

Located in the heart of Abuja, XM Unisex Salon & Spa offers expert hairstyling, luxurious spa treatments, and premium beauty services for all genders. Whether you're here for a quick touch-up or a full-day pampering experience, our skilled professionals deliver top-notch care in a clean, relaxing, and friendly environment.

We believe beauty is personal — so we listen, advise, and tailor each service to suit your unique needs. From precise cuts and stunning braids to rejuvenating facials, massages, and human hair wig installations, Annish is your trusted destination for head-to-toe self-care.
